About Us

Dartmouth Laundromat is a family-owned business located at 710 State Road in North Dartmouth, MA. We offer self-service laundry, wash/dry/fold, dry cleaning, and alterations. Our facility is clean, spacious, and fully attended, with ample parking and modern machines.

Services

  • Self-Service Washers & Dryers
  • Wash/Dry/Fold Drop-Off
  • Dry Cleaning
  • Comforter & Bedding Cleaning

Wash & Read Program

We proudly host a “Wash and Read” corner with free children’s books in English, Spanish, and Portuguese. This initiative, in partnership with United Way’s Women United, promotes early literacy while families do laundry.
